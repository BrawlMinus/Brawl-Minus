Top Secret:
Check out All-Star Versus in Special Brawl (Just don't choose Link unless he's first.) - Don't forget to thank wiiztec.
Restart a match out of pause (this won't work in All-Star Versus), here are the inputs:

During Pause Hold
GameCube Controller: L-R-A-B-START/PAUSE
Classic Controller: L-R-A-B-START
Wii Remote + Nunchuck: Z-C-B-1-PLUS OR Z-B-1-MINUS-PLUS
Wii Remote: B-1-2-MINUS-PLUS

Upon seeing "GAME" Hold
GameCube Controller: L-R-B
Classic Controller: L-R-B
Wii Remote + Nunchuck: Z-C OR PLUS-MINUS
Wii Remote: PLUS-MINUS

Return to CSS from SSS by Holding
GameCube Controller: L-R-A
Classic Controller: L-R-A
Wii Remote + Nunchuck: Z-B-1
Wii Remote: B-1-2
